A Litte something about US

Welcome to the UMBRELL-A-HACK Hackathon Challenge, where innovation meets creativity and problem-solving knows no bounds! In this exhilarating event, talented individuals from diverse backgrounds come together to tackle real-world issues through technology and ingenuity. Over the course of the next few days, participants will immerse themselves in a whirlwind of coding, ideation, and collaboration, all with the aim of creating groundbreaking solutions. Our hackathon is not just a competition; it's a platform for aspiring innovators to push their limits, learn, and build something remarkable. So, whether you're a coding wizard, a design genius, or a problem-solving enthusiast, get ready to embark on a journey of innovation and make a lasting impact. The Hackathon Challenge awaits your brilliance!
